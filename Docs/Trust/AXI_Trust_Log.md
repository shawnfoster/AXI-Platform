# AXI Trust Log

This file is intentionally separate from repository governance.

It is informational only.

It does not authorize implementation, milestones, ADRs, constitutional
changes, or repository-governed work.

Repository governance remains authoritative.

---

# Purpose

Capture executive observations, strategic ideas, product concepts, UX
improvements, research candidates, business opportunities, and future
capabilities that emerge during development but are not yet
repository-authorized.

These entries preserve potentially valuable thinking without widening
current repository scope.

---

# Entry Format

Each entry preserves:

- Date
- Category
- Context
- Observation
- Why it matters
- Potential future impact
- Suggested constitutional path

---

# Entries

## 2026-07-19 — Future Capability

**Context**

M23 closeout and Executive Trust instruction.

**Observation**

AXI would benefit from a first-class operational "Idea Incubator"
surface that captures executive insights, strategic ideas, product
concepts, and future opportunities without treating them as approved
governance.

**Why it matters**

The repository currently separates approved governance from
conversation, but it does not yet publish an operational surface for
preserving out-of-scope executive insight in a structured, reusable,
and reviewable way.

**Potential future impact**

- improve strategic memory across sessions
- preserve high-value ideas without contaminating constitutional
  authority
- create a governed promotion path from idea to review candidate to
  milestone proposal
- strengthen AXI's long-term knowledge operationalization posture

**Suggested constitutional path**

If later adopted, treat the Idea Incubator as a future operational
surface rather than a constitutional primitive.

Do not introduce it through conversational practice alone.

Require explicit future assignment, publish the operational boundary
that separates incubator entries from repository authority, and then
define any needed objects, workflows, or milestone work only through
later approved governance.
