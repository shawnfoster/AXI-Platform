# Purpose

Publish the architecture and canonical table of contents for the AXI
Field Manual.

The Field Manual is the governed applied-practice layer that converts
Decision Intelligence methodology, organizational context, and governed
expertise into repeatable playbooks for real operating scenarios.

---

# Architectural Role

The Field Manual:

- operationalizes approved decision methodology
- preserves explicit links to evidence, context, assumptions,
  constraints, and readiness
- connects field playbooks back to governed expertise and the
  Organizational Digital Twin
- captures how practical engagements should feed lessons and expertise
  updates rather than remain isolated know-how

The Field Manual shall not:

- invent new platform capabilities
- bypass human approval
- assert regulatory conclusions
- replace the Operating Manual or the governing ADRs

# Relationship to Decision Intelligence

Every field-manual playbook shall explicitly connect back to:

- the canonical decision lifecycle
- the relevant capability set
- required evidence and provenance expectations
- operating context, readiness, and regulatory knowledge where
  applicable
- lesson capture and expertise improvement

No field-manual playbook may be treated as governed expertise unless it
preserves that traceability chain.
