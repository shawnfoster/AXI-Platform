# AXI Approval Policy v1.0

**Document ID:** AXI-AP-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Founder Review  
**Lifecycle:** Draft

## Purpose
Define the authority, responsibilities, and approval process for governed reconstruction decisions.

## Approval Principles
1. No governed object becomes canonical without approval.
2. Approvals are recorded permanently.
3. Approval authority is explicit.
4. Revisions require a new approval event.

## Approval Authorities
### Founder
Final authority for Phase 0 governance and Baseline v1.0.

### Future Governance Board
May review and recommend approval when established. Founder authority remains unless delegated.

## Approval States
- Draft
- Under Review
- Approved
- Rejected
- Revision Requested

## Required Approval Record
- Document ID
- Version
- Decision
- Approver
- Date
- Rationale
- Related Decision Record

## Rules
- Rejections are retained with rationale.
- Superseded approvals remain archived.
- Approval history is immutable.

## Definition of Completion
A governed object satisfies this policy when its approval history is complete, auditable, and linked to the governing decision.
